# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account Management

### How do I create an account?
To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and fill in your email address, password, and basic information. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

### How do I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to set a new password

### How do I update my profile information?
To update your profile:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Account Settings
3. Click "Edit Profile"
4. Make your changes and save

## Billing and Subscriptions

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual subscriptions.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription:
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Click on "Billing & Subscription"
3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
4. Follow the confirmation steps
Note: Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. Contact our support team within 30 days of your initial purchase for a full refund.

### Do you offer student discounts?
Yes! We offer a 50% student discount. Verify your student status through our partner verification service in your account settings.

## Technical Support

### Why is the app running slowly?
If the app is running slowly:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try using a different browser
4. Restart your device
5. Contact support if the issue persists

### The app keeps crashing, what should I do?
For app crashes:
1. Force close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest app version
4. Clear app cache (Android) or reinstall (iOS)
5. Contact support with your device details

### How do I sync my data across devices?
Data syncing is automatic when you're logged in. Make sure you're using the same account on all devices and have a stable internet connection.

## Features and Usage

### How do I export my data?
To export your data:
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Click "Data Export"
3. Select the data you want to export
4. Choose your preferred format (CSV, JSON, PDF)
5. Click "Generate Export"
You'll receive an email when your export is ready for download.

### Is my data secure?

- We never sell your personal data
